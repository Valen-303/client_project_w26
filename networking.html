Networking & Connections

Building Meaningful Professional Relationships

In the information field, building a strong professional network is as essential as mastering technical skills. By engaging with alumni, industry experts, and mentors, you can gain valuable career insights and uncover hidden job opportunities. We encourage students to actively utilize LinkedIn and the various social platforms provided by UMSI to stay connected.

UMSI Networking Resources

The UMSI Career Development Office (CDO) regularly hosts career salons and recruitment fairs to bridge the gap between students and the industry. Additionally, the 'Career Link' platform provides direct access to top employers. Be sure to keep your professional profile updated and prepare your 30-second elevator pitch for these networking opportunities.
